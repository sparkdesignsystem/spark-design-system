# Space

The distance between elements and how these proportions interact in the design.

## Usage
** Padding and margin are relative to the base size of `16px` or `1em`.**

Establish visual cohesion by applying consistent spacing in layouts and components.

Apply these proportions through:
- [Box](/using-spark/components/box)
- [Stack](/using-spark/components/stack)
- [Spacing Utilities](/using-spark/foundations/css-utilities#spacing)

### Guidelines
- There are five built in spacing sizes:
    - Extra Small (`4px`, `.25rem`)
    - Small (`8px`, `.5rem`)
    - Medium (`16px`, `1rem`)
    - Large (`32px`, `2rem`)
    - Extra Large (`64px`, `4rem`)

## Variants

### Inset

Adds equal padding to all four sides of an element.

> For illustrative purposes, gray represents the padding of the element. It will not appear in your project.

<ComponentPreview
  componentName="spacing--inset-spacing"

  hasHTML
/>

### Inset Short

Similar to [Inset](#inset), except the
top and bottom padding is reduced by 50%.

For example, Inset Short Small has `8px` of padding
on the left and right sides, but `4px` padding
on the top and bottom.

> For illustrative purposes, gray represents the padding of the element. It will not appear in your project.

example, Inset Tall Small has `8px` padding on the
left and right sides, but `12px` padding on the
top and bottom.
